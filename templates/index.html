  • NMeras

    What is NMeras

    NMeras is an AI-powered platform that assists nurses in formulating PICO (Population, Intervention, Comparison, Outcome) questions, searching for relevant evidence, and conducting critical appraisal of research studies, thereby streamlining the evidence based practice (EBP) process.

  • Mission

    To empower nurses with AI-driven tools and resources to enhance evidence-based practice (EBP), therefore improving patient outcomes and advancing healthcare practices.

    Mission

  • Vision

    To revolutionize nursing practice by providing accessible, intuitive, and AI-powered support for evidence-based decision-making, fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation within healthcare.

WHY NMeras?

Your ultimate tool for evidence-based research and appraisal.

Enter Your PICO Question

Simply input your clinical or research question following the PICO format (Population, Intervention, Comparison, Outcome), and we'll retrieve the most relevant studies for you.

Retrieve and Rank Studies

We use the Oxford appraisal tool to rank studies by relevance and quality, ensuring you access the best evidence for your clinical decisions.

Summarize and Present Results

Get summarized insights from the research, helping you make data-driven decisions quickly and efficiently for your clinical practice or research.
